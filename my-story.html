My Story

Looking Forward... Fall 2024 As I continue to get more confident in my coding abilities and expand my portfolio I look forward to gaining real-world industry experience outside the classroom. I am actively seeking internships in Software Development across the United States, where I am confident that I can make valuable contributions and a meaningful impact. I am enthusiastic about the prospect of contributing to the software industry's growth and advancement.
Began my Master's in Software Development August 2023 - Present In August 2023 I began my 16-month MSD program. The program was specifically designed for people who did not have a background in Computer Science, and is expedited to produce fully competent software engineers in less than a year and a half. This has been by far the most challenging thing I have ever done, but that's what I was looking for when I left Hospitality. I am grateful to have found this career, and I am excited to continue to be able to push myself every day as I keep up with a quickly changing and growing industry.
Traveled to South America April 2023 At the end of my time at the Montage Resort I went on a three-week trip to South America. My property had been newly opened and because of this, had hired nearly all of their frontline staff from an international work and travel program. My whole team for the two F&B outlets I managed were from Argentina and Brazil. After sharing time with them in the USA and getting to share pieces of our culture with them (South Americans do NOT like rootbeer floats) I went down to visit them to appreciate their culture. Getting to travel internationally opened up my eyes to what I wanted to see and do in my life, and it was while I was in Brazil that I got accepted to the Master's program that I am currently enrolled in.
Worked in Hospitality but Needed a Change May 2021 - April 2023 My 2 years working for Marriott were full of experiences I will always be grateful for. I met people from all walks of life, made friends for life, but most importantly, gained clarity about my career path. Having accelerated through high school and college to enter the workforce early, I had envisioned continuous growth and fulfillment in Hospitality. However, as I delved deeper into roles spanning the Front Desk, Housekeeping, and Food and Beverage departments, I began to feel a sense of monotony. I hungered for challenges that would push me to excel and opportunities to learn something new each day. It was while I was a Food and Beverage Manager at a Montage Resort that I discovered the University of Utah's Master of Software Development program and decided to apply.
Graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Hospitality Management January 2019 - May 2021 After graduating from high school I moved to Utah to continue my education at Utah Valley University. In 2021, I graduated with a Bachelor's of Science Degree in Hospitality Management. I graduated Summa Cum Laude and was awarded the "Outstanding Student" award of my graduating class. I had been working at a Marriott Vacation Club resort in Park City while completing my degree and within a week of my graduation I had moved to Palm Springs, California, to continue my career with the company.
Where it All Started February 2001 - January 2019 I grew up in Seattle, Washington and attended Issaquah High School, class of 2019. While in my formative years I developed a love for reading, soccer, baking, and spending time with my four brothers. I am grateful to have grown up in the PNW and always look forward to returning home to visit friends and family.
